About Tomato Leaf Disease Detection

This application uses a deep learning model based on EfficientNetB3 with CBAM (Convolutional Block Attention Module) to detect diseases in tomato leaves. The model was trained on a dataset of tomato leaf images and can detect 10 different conditions, including various diseases and healthy leaves.

Detectable Conditions:

  • Bacterial Spot
  • Early Blight
  • Late Blight
  • Leaf Mold
  • Septoria Leaf Spot
  • Spider Mites
  • Target Spot
  • Yellow Leaf Curl Virus
  • Mosaic Virus
  • Healthy

For more information on these diseases and how to manage them, please consult with a professional agriculturist or refer to trusted agricultural resources.
